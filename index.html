Hafzan Equity Composite Index (HECI)

The Hafzan Equity Composite Index (HECI) is a quarterly-updated benchmark tracking India's top 23 Shariah-compliant companies. Companies are selected through a rigorous screening process focused on ethical compliance, financial health, and sustainability. The index was officially launched in Q2 2025 with a base value of 1000.

Inclusion Criteria

  • Excluded: banking, alcohol, gambling, and other non-compliant sectors
  • Debt-to-Market Cap < 33%, Non-halal revenue < 5%
  • Minimum Market Cap: ₹8300 Cr | Avg. Daily Volume (3 months): ≥ 100,000 shares
  • Profitable in at least 2 of the last 3 fiscal years
  • No major ESG violations, fraud investigations, or delisting risks
  • Diversified: At least 3 industries represented, max 30% weight from any single industry
  • Minimum score of 60/100 on internal fundamental scorecard

Key Features

  • 100% Shariah-compliant equity index
  • Tracks India's top 23 qualifying listed companies
  • Rebalanced every quarter
  • Performance benchmarked against Nifty 50 and S&P 500
  • Launched with a base value of 1000 in April 2025
  • No single stock has more than 10% weight in the index
